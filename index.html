About the app

Tempora is a simple timesheet solution, which allows your team to easily record time worked at the push of a button. Tempora streamlines your time tracking for client facing or internal work and enables leave booking on the move as well. All data instantly synchronises with our desktop product, which provides expanded functionality and over 100 reports.

Time recording:

- Quickly record time at the end of the day
- Use a timer for real-time, time recording
- Update or delete time recordings as needed
- View time by day, week or month
- Record time at a granular or high level
- Add notes to time for greater accountability
- Timesheets can be approved
- Set minimum required hours per day or week

Absence:
- Track sickness, annual leave and all other forms of absence
- Instant access to up to date holiday entitlement
- Complete self-service tool
- Notifications of holiday request status

Tempora Reports:

- Report on time, cost, profit, budgets, utilisation, absence and account over-servicing
- Automated reports direct to your E-mail inbox
- Exportable to Excel
- Report customisation tools, allow you to break the data into how you need to see it

**A subscription to the Tempora service is required, free trial available. More at www.temporasoftware.com**
